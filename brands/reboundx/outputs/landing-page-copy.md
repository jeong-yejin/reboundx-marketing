## 5. FAQ

**Q: What exactly is a fee rebate?**
When you trade on a partner exchange, you pay trading fees. ReboundX gets a referral share from the exchange and passes a large portion of it back to you automatically.

**Q: Does this affect my trading at all?**
Not at all. Your trades, your strategy, your platform — nothing changes. ReboundX operates entirely in the background.

**Q: When do I get paid?**
Within 24 hours of each trade. Rebates show up on your dashboard in real time and are withdrawable once settled.

**Q: I already have an exchange account. Can I still use ReboundX?**
Only if you originally signed up through a ReboundX referral link. If not, you'll need to create a new account through us — we provide step-by-step guides for every exchange.

**Q: Is this available for Perp DEX traders?**
Yes. We support 6 Perp DEXs — the widest coverage in the industry. You can also use our Perpdex comparison tool and airdrop calculator at reboundx.io/en/perpdex.
