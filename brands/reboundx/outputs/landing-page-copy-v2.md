## 3. How Fee Rebates Work

**Section Title:** Your Fee Doesn't Disappear. Part of It Comes Back.

**Explainer block (3-column):**

**What you currently do:**
Pay trading fees to your exchange. Those fees fund the exchange's referral program. Most traders never see that money again.

**What ReboundX does:**
We hold a referral partnership with each exchange. When you trade through our link, the exchange pays us a referral share. We pass the majority of that share directly to you.

**What changes:**
Nothing about your trading changes. Same exchange, same strategy, same positions. ReboundX operates in the background and settles automatically.

**Supporting line:**
This isn't a bonus. It's a structural fee return that exists whether you claim it or not.

## 9. FAQ

**Q: I already have an account on OKX / Bybit / Binance. Can I still get rebates?**
Only if you originally registered through a ReboundX referral link. If your existing account isn't linked to our referral, it can't be connected retroactively. In that case, you'd need to create a new account through our link. We provide step-by-step guides for each exchange — including what to do if you already have an account.

**Q: What exactly is a fee rebate? Is this a bonus or a reward?**
It's neither. Your trading fees are partly used to fund exchange referral programs. ReboundX holds a referral partnership with each exchange, and we pass the majority of that referral share back to you as a fee return. You were paying fees either way. This is a portion of that cost coming back.

**Q: Does using ReboundX affect my trading in any way?**
No. Your trades, your strategy, your pairs — nothing changes. ReboundX doesn't interact with your trading activity.

**Q: When do rebates get paid?**
Within 24 hours of each qualifying trade. Settlements appear on your dashboard in real time. Once settled, they're withdrawable immediately.

**Q: How do I know the settlement amounts are accurate?**
Every settlement record is published on-chain. You can independently verify the amounts without relying on ReboundX's dashboard.

**Q: I've heard of platforms that promise rebates but never pay out. How is this different?**
ReboundX publishes on-chain settlement records for every payout. You're not relying on our word — you can verify each transaction independently. We've also run live trading competitions and community events with 400+ attendees, which involves direct coordination with partner exchanges. We're not anonymous.

**Q: Are Perp DEX rebates handled differently from CEX rebates?**
The payout structure is similar — you connect your DEX wallet, trade as usual, and rebates settle within 24 hours. Some Perp DEXs offer zero-fee structures or airdrop incentives instead of direct rebate rates. We explain the specifics for each exchange in our partner guide.
